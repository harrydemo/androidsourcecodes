Creatures

During your battle against the forces of evil you will face many different kind of enemies. As the game goes on the enemies will get tougher and harder to defeat. On top of that your enemies use black magic to gain special abilities, so spend your money wisely or else your garden will fall into the cruel hands of the rabbit-army.

Special abilities:

Fast: These creatures will move accross the map 50% faster than ordinary creatures.

Fire resistant: Will only take the regular damage from a tower with fire damage.

Frost resistant: Can't be slowed by frost towers.

Poison resistant: Will not be affected by damage over time.

Levels:

Map2 contains wave 1-35.
Map3 contains wave 1-40.
Map4 contains wave 1-45.
