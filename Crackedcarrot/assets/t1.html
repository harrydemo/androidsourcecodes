Bunker

This tower fires shurikens in fast rate on a single target. The tower is available in the following types:

Lvl 1:
 Price: 8g
Resell value: 4g
Damage: 7-9
Towerspeed: Fast
Range: 100

Lvl 2:
Upgradeprice: 22g
Resell value: 15g
Damage: 21-27
Towerspeed: Fast
Range: 110

Lvl 3:
Upgradeprice: 58g
Resell value: 48g
Damage: 63-81
Towerspeed: Fast
Range: 120

This tower also has special abilities avaible when tower is upgraded to level 2. The abilities will remain if you upgrade the tower.

Lvl3 90g: (Minimum damage of tower*1)/s for 4 sec
Lvl4 180g: (Minimum damage of tower*2)/s for 4 sec
Lvl5 360g: (Minimum damage of tower*4)/s for 4 sec

Fire:
